Our Dogs

Finding forever homes for dogs

Furry Friends Shelter was founded in 2020 to find forever homes for dogs. We are here to help you find your new best friend. With our lovely staff and volunteers we are dedicated to providing a safe haven for dogs in need.

We need your help

Our team works tirelessly to rehabilitate and socialize our furry friends, ensuring they are ready for their forever home. But we can´t do it alone. Your donation can make a significant difference in our dogs lives. It will go towards food, vaccinations and other medical treatments.
