Our Dogs

Find your new best friend here

We receive many dogs looking for a new home. We make sure to give them enough time to arrive in our shelter before putting them up for adoption.
All dogs are spayed/neutered, have a microchip and have all required vaccines. If they have any health problems we will make sure the new owner knows about them and can handle the dog correctly.

If you are interested in one of the dogs contact us or visit us during our opening hours.

Look for your new best friend below!
