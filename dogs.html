Our Dogs

Find your new best friend here

We recieve many dogs looking for a new home. We make sure to give them enough time to arrive in our shelter before putting them up for adoption.
All dogs are spayed/neutered, have a microchip and all required vaccines. If they have any health problems we will make sure the new owner knows about them and is able to handle the dog correctly.

Look for your new best friend below!

male | 4 years | Mix Pino is a friendly male Podenco mix. He loves walks and playing with other dogs. But he loves chilling on the couch and looking out of the window just as much. He is good with other dogs and children but doesn´t like cats.

male | 2 years | Beagle Pauli is an active male Beagle. He loves running laps, so a big garden would be ideal. He is good with other dogs, cats and children.
